Notes for this v0.1

  • Brand-deferred placeholders brand pass — the hourglass + "Zugzwang Verified" lockup, and the zugzwangworld.com source line in the bottom margin. Both get finalised when the logo is locked; the source line is the pull-back-to-platform mechanism, so it holds the space now.
